About Us

At DragonGlass Window Cleaning, we’re not just cleaning windows — we’re building something special, one shine at a time. We’re a small, family-run business founded by a husband-and-wife duo with a passion for quality, integrity, and making every home or business look its best. What started as a simple dream quickly turned into a hands-on mission: to bring unbeatable shine and personal service to our local community. With just a squeegee, a bucket, and a big vision, we set out to create a company that customers could count on — not just for streak-free glass, but for honest communication, flexible scheduling, and reliable results. Whether we’re restoring the sparkle to your storefront or helping your home feel brighter and more inviting, we treat every window like it’s our own. You’ll always get friendly service, professional work, and the kind of care that only comes from a team that truly values your trust. As DragonGlass grows, we remain proudly grounded in our core values: Family First Work Done Right Customers Treated Like Neighbors We believe clean windows do more than improve curb appeal — they let the light in. And we’re here to make that happen for you.
